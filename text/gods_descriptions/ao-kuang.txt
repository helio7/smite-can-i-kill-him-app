            AO KUANG

   The ability 2 gives you 6 dragons. Every time you hit a basic attack, a dragon will attack the enemy.
   You can throw the remaining dragons with the ability 3.

   You can select the number of dragons used in your melee basic attacks using the "Melee dra:" input box. 6 maximum.

   Example: you choose 4. The damage of the ability 2 will be calculated with 4 melee dragons. The damage of the ability 3 will be calculated with 2 dragons.
